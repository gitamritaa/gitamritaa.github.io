About Srila Prabhupada

His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada appeared in 1896 in Calcutta, India. He dedicated his life to spreading Krishna consciousness all over the world.

His Mission

At the age of 69, Srila Prabhupada traveled alone to the West. In 1966, he founded the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON).

Literary Contributions

Srila Prabhupada translated and commented on Bhagavad-gita As It Is, Srimad Bhagavatam, and Chaitanya Charitamrita.
