Biography

His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada was born in 1896 in Calcutta, India. He was a pure devotee of Lord Krishna and the founder-acharya of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON).

At the age of 69, Srila Prabhupada traveled to the United States with the mission to spread Krishna consciousness throughout the world. Despite many difficulties, he established temples, translated sacred Vedic scriptures, and inspired millions to follow the path of bhakti-yoga.
